Average Cost of Modern Damascus Steel

Damascus steel is some of the finest forged steel you can produce or buy on the market today. It has a reputation for being both durable and beautiful. Damascus steel is essentially layered steel that is forged together to create different patterns. Damascus steel can be very expensive depending on the pattern you choose and the size of your billet.
