Educator Application Supervision

Brigham Young University

Project Overview

TLDR

Provide a second level of communication to project partners to assure project completion and momentum

Provide consultation, mentoring, and experiential learning to student UX designers

Supervise a UX team of 4 student UX designers

Report progress to my director and maintain a good accounting of project progress

The Project

Educator is an application created to aid the Department of Education to track student progress and certification as well as meet accreditation standards for education.

I was assigned to supervise a team of 4 student UX designers over a period of 4 months and report project progress to my director to assure we were meeting our teamsâ€™ standards and the needs of our partners and users.

One of our goals as a UX team is to provide experiential learning for our student UX team members. I provided guidance and advice to our student designers when they had questions and needed help. We held weekly meetings as a UX team as well as weekly agile meetings with engineers to make sure we were on task and accomplishing our goals.

At times, I provided a higher level of communication for our partners to assure them that we were hearing their needs and working to research, design, test, and modify different application functions and sections.

Several features of the Educator application are already in full production and being used by campus partners to improve what used to be many manual processes and third-party applications.
