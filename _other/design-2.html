 MASTER OF STATISTICS

Welcome to the Master of Statistics program at the University of Utah - an interdepartmental and interdisciplinary program that has a rich history from 1976. The program awards the professional MStat degree with concentration areas in Biostatistics, Econometrics, Educational Psychology, Mathematics, and Sociology. In the program you will have the opportunity to learn the latest in statistics and probability theory and learn about state-of-the-art programming and applications in your chosen area of concentration.
