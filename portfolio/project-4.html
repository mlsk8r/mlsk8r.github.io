SUU Website Redesign

UX Research, prototpying, and testing

Project Overview

Desktop View

TLDR

Complete redesign of the look, feel, and user experience of the SUU website

Develop wireframes and layouts on paper in collaboration with marketing team

Develop prototypes in Adobe XD

Develop rapid prototype web pages for fast review and feedback

Review, redesign, and develop new prototypes to test new ideas and specific features

The Project

SUU marketing and web services have been working together and collaborating on better branding, design, and user experience. To help begin this process, there has been much research and development into new design ideas, user experience layout, and functionality.

The initial phase of the process involved developing user goals and creating low-fidelity wireframes and layouts on paper. After putting pen to paper, rapid prototypes were developed in adobe XD and small concepts and features were explored using prototypes as a guide and user tests. To help aid pre-visualization, web page prototypes were also developed to explore further and test user experience, design, and interaction ideas and concepts.

Web prototypes were developed, reviewed, and reworked to implement a new idea or concept to test. The current test pages are still in the testing and experimentation phase and new ideas and prototypes are being designed and developed.
