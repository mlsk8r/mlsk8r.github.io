BYUI Account Sign-in Has a New Look!

The parent organization of BYU is the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

Account sign-in now redirects to the churchofjesuschrist.org sign in page. Sign in with your Church Account username and password. You will then be redirected back to BYUI's website.

Don't have a Church Account? No problem!

Click the "Sign Up" link on the Church's sign-in page and follow the steps to create a new account.
