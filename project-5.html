The Project

This was a recruitment landing page contracted by the Admissions department with a very tight deadline. The page was designed and developed from start to finish by me in collaboration with a graphic designer. The goal of the project was to promote high school recruitment and the first-year student journey. Mockups were designed and shared between the design and web development departments. Quick web prototypes were developed to show designers the potential of the design mockups and how they would transition to responsive layouts on the web. Collaboration with designers and stakeholders was key throughout the development process before launching the final product/site. Project stakeholders and recruiters were pleased with the final outcome and the use of this new page as a tool for recruitment and admissions.
