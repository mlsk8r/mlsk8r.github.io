SGU Regional Airport Logo Redesign and Brand Package

This project was a case study into redesigning the logo and business package for the SGU Regional Airport. There was a lot of sketching and brainstorming about different logos and logotype designs for this project. I received a lot of feedback and constructive criticism from people about my sketches and mockups before settling on the final design and colors.
