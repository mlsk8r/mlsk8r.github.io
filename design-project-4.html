Logo Creation and Branding for Mock Company "Global Group"

This project was a quick 1 hour case study into the creation of a brand identity and logo for a mock company called Global Group. I had a basic description about the company product and goals and had one hour to design a logo that fit the company persona and feel based on that information. Sketches were shared with other artists and individuals and after feedback and final design was settled upon as well as a simple color scheme.
