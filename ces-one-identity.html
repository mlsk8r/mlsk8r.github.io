The Project

BYU Provo is part of several institutes that form the Church Education System (CES). Under the parent umbrella of CES, a team has been put together at BYU Provo to solve problems with user identity across all CES institutes. Our team works with other “working groups” that represent each institute under CES.

I am the teams lead UX/UI designer and researcher. I have been working with the CES 1-Identity Team for almost 2 years now.

I have been tasked with providing UX/UI consultatiion, design consultation, user research, surveys, diagrams, mockups, user flows, and various other multimedia design needs to help further the project goals and plans.

Currently the project is focusing on providing users with a single user login experience that would be united across all CES institutes.
