The Project

BYU Provo is part of several institutes that form the Church Education System (CES). Under the parent umbrella of CES, a team has been put together at BYU Provo to solve problems with user identity across all CES institutes. Our team works with other “working groups” that represent each institute under CES.

My services were offered as a UX/UI consultant and team member for the CES One Identity project.

The project is still in the beginning stages of research and development. I have been tasked with providing UX consultatiion, design consultation, user research, surveys, diagrams, mockups, user flows, and various other multimedia design needs to help further the project goals and plans.

Currently the project is focusing on providing users with a single user login experience that would be united across all CES institutes. I am working on developing user flows and mockups concepts for the various ways this user experience could be accomplished and tested.
