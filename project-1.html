The Project

The Enrollment Marketing & Communication department at the University of Arizona wanted to redesign and restructure their website from an outdated look and structure. As a web designer/developer, collaboration was done closely with graphic designers and other team members. Feedback and consultation was provided about how structure, layout, and designs would transfer to actual web layout and development across desktop and mobile devices. The initial mockups were only created for desktop layout. After collaboration, testing, and fun teamwork, mockups were created for responsive mobile layouts and to create the best user experience.

Throughout the design process, rapid prototypes were created to help show the team and stakeholders what would be possible to develop when transitioning the designs to actual web pages and site structure. Constant communication and teamwork were necessary to deliver the project on time and keep within the scope of the initial goals and designs. After an initial soft launch, phase II plans were made to continue to develop the site and apply new ideas based on user feedback and new ideas.
